> overview
Welcome to Dungeon4Fun
A top-down, wave-based action roguelite. Survive, upgrade, and climb the leaderboards!
This site is a forever work in progress... click around for secrets! (´｡• ᵕ •｡`)
> features
  • Wave-based combat: Survive increasingly difficult enemy waves.
  • RPG progression: Level up and select from random upgrade cards.
  • Weapon variety: Multiple ranged and melee weapons, each with unique mechanics.
  • Save/load system: Save your run in one of three slots.
  • Online/offline play: Play with or without backend connectivity.
  • Leaderboards: Compete for high scores online.
  • Feedback system: Send feedback directly from the game.
  • In-game encyclopedia: View upgrades, weapons, and enemy info.
